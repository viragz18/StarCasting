Who We Are?

We are team of Northeastern University and dedicated towards web development. In the increasing competency of movie industry, we had thought to make platform where a potential actor or actress can show their talent to directors. While at the same time any registered directors can review the talent portfolio and directly contact the actor or actress. Through this platform Directors can reduce the time and cost of audition set up and can review on the go. While an aspiring actor or actress can be benefited as they can directly show their talent and can get a break in the industry
